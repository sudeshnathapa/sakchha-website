Sakchha is a Nepali service platform that helps businesses explore, build, and operate offshore teams. Its main focus to “remove boundaries create a global world defines by successful remote work and make Nepal a back office of the world. n this time wherein the difficulty of the pandemic is increasing, and everyone is facing issues with health, business, travel, job, and day-to-day end-meet, we thought this is the perfect way to connect Nepal to the rest of the world and provide awareness that various skills in Nepal can be hired remotely by organizations and individuals outside of Nepal.
